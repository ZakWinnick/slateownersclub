About Us

The Slate Owners Club is the first community for future Slate drivers—owners, builders, and tinkerers who believe the best trucks are the ones you make your own.

As we count down to the launch of the Blank Slate, we're bringing together early adopters and DIY enthusiasts ready to shape a new kind of electric ownership experience—simple, customizable, and unapologetically hands-on.

Whether you're already planning your first mods or just curious about what's ahead, this is where the journey begins.

"Built Simple. Made Yours."
